FC Barcelona's Trophies

La Liga

Total Wins: 27 titles

FC Barcelona has won the prestigious La Liga title 27 times, with their most recent title in the 2018-2019 season.

Copa del Rey

Total Wins: 31 titles

Barcelona holds the record for the most Copa del Rey victories, with 31 wins to their name.

Supercopa de españa

Total Wins: 14 titles

The Supercopa de España is contested between the winners of La Liga and Copa del Rey, and Barcelona has dominated this competition over the years.

UEFA Champions League

Total Wins: 5 titles

Barcelona has won the prestigious UEFA Champions League five times, with some of the most memorable victories in 2006, 2009, 2011, and 2015.

FIFA Club World Cup

Total Wins: 3 titles

FC Barcelona has claimed the FIFA Club World Cup three times, competing against the best teams from around the world.

UEFA Super Cup

Total Wins: 5 titles

Barcelona has lifted the UEFA Super Cup five times, claiming the trophy by defeating the UEFA Champions League winners.

Copa de la Liga

Total Wins: 2 titles

Although no longer contested, FC Barcelona has won the Copa de la Liga twice during its history.

Inter-Cities Fairs Cup

Total Wins: 3 titles

FC Barcelona won the Inter-Cities Fairs Cup, a precursor to the UEFA Cup, on three occasions during its history.

Joan Gamper Trophy

Total Wins: Multiple (Annual Tournament)

This friendly competition is held annually in honor of the club's founder, Joan Gamper, and is one of Barcelona's cherished traditions.
